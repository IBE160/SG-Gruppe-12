2. Identify required skills, qualifications, and experience

â±ï¸ **Processing time:** 10-20 seconds

#### Step 3: Review Results

### Understanding Your Match Score

**Match Score (0-100%):**
- Shows how well your CV aligns with the job requirements
- Based on keyword matching and skill analysis

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **80-100%** | Excellent match | Apply confidently! |
| **60-79%** | Good match | Consider applying, emphasize matching skills |
| **40-59%** | Moderate match | Apply if interested, but highlight transferable skills |
| **0-39%** | Weak match | Consider upskilling or targeting different roles |

### Understanding Your ATS Score

**ATS Score (0-100):**
- Measures how well your CV will perform in Applicant Tracking Systems
- Based on formatting, keyword density, and structure

| Score | Rating | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **85-100** | Excellent | Your CV is highly ATS-optimized |
| **70-84** | Good | Minor improvements recommended |
| **50-69** | Fair | Needs optimization for better ATS performance |
| **0-49** | Poor | Significant improvements needed |

### ATS Score Breakdown

Your ATS score is calculated from:

1. **Keyword Density (40%):**
   - Presence of job-relevant keywords
   - Natural keyword usage (not stuffed)

2. **Formatting Quality (30%):**
   - Clean, parseable structure
   - Standard section headings
   - No complex tables or graphics

3. **Section Completeness (20%):**
   - All expected sections present
   - Sufficient detail in each section

4. **Quantifiable Achievements (10%):**
   - Use of numbers and metrics
   - Concrete results and impact

### Keyword Analysis

#### Present Keywords âœ…

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Upload Failed" Error

**Possible causes:**
- File is too large (>5MB)
- File format not supported (only PDF/TXT)
- Network connection issue

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size and format
2. Try compressing your PDF
3. Convert DOCX to PDF before uploading
4. Check your internet connection
5. Try again in a few minutes

#### "AI Processing Failed" Error

**Possible causes:**
- AI service temporarily unavailable
- Rate limit exceeded
- Invalid input data

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 1 minute and try again
2. Check that your job description is at least 10 characters
3. Ensure you're not making too many requests (limit: 10 per 15 minutes)
4. Contact support if issue persists

#### CV Parsing Errors

**Problem:** AI didn't extract my CV correctly

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure your CV has clear section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
2. Use standard formatting (no complex tables or graphics)
3. Try converting to plain text first
4. Manually edit any incorrect sections after parsing

#### "Session Expired" Message

**Cause:** You've been inactive for 15 minutes

**Solution:**
1. Click "Log In" again
2. Enter your credentials
3. Your unsaved changes may be lost - always ensure autosave is working

#### Low Match Score

**Problem:** My match score is lower than expected

**Possible reasons:**
1. Your CV is missing keywords from the job posting
2. Your experience doesn't align with requirements
3. Your CV needs better keyword optimization

**Solutions:**
1. Review "Missing Keywords" section
2. Add relevant skills if you have them
3. Tailor your CV for this specific role
4. Use the same language as the job posting

#### Low ATS Score

**Problem:** My ATS score is below 70

**Common issues:**
1. **Complex formatting:** Remove tables, text boxes, headers/footers
2. **Missing sections:** Add Education, Skills, Experience sections
3. **Insufficient keywords:** Use more job-relevant terms
4. **Lack of quantifiable achievements:** Add numbers and metrics

**Solutions:**
1. Review ATS suggestions
2. Simplify your CV format
3. Use standard section headings
4. Add quantifiable achievements (increased sales by 25%, managed team of 5, etc.)

### Performance Issues

#### Slow Loading

**Solutions:**
1. Clear browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet speed
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Contact support if slowness persists

#### Timeout Errors

**Cause:** AI processing taking too long

**Solutions:**
1. Shorten your job description (under 5000 characters)
2. Try again in a few minutes
3. Check your internet connection

## Appendix: Best Practices

### CV Writing Tips

1. **Use keywords from the job posting** - But naturally, not keyword stuffing
2. **Quantify achievements** - "Increased sales by 25%" vs "Increased sales"
3. **Use action verbs** - Led, Managed, Developed, Implemented, etc.
4. **Keep it concise** - 1-2 pages maximum
5. **Use standard formatting** - Simple, clean, ATS-friendly design
6. **Tailor for each job** - Customize your CV for each application

### ATS Optimization Tips

1. **Use standard section headings** - Experience, Education, Skills
2. **Avoid tables and graphics** - Use simple text formatting
3. **Include a skills section** - List relevant technical and soft skills
4. **Use standard fonts** - Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman
5. **Save as PDF** - Preserves formatting across systems
6. **Match job title keywords** - Use the same terms as the job posting

### Job Application Strategy

1. **Research the company** - Understand their culture and values
2. **Tailor your CV** - Highlight relevant experience for each role
3. **Write a strong cover letter** - Explain why you're a great fit
4. **Follow up** - Send a polite follow-up email after 1-2 weeks
5. **Track applications** - Use our saved analyses to track your applications
